# AFRA Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AFRA Visibility, LLC ("AFRA," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and discloses personal data when you use our software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform (the "Service"). This policy applies to both our business customers ("Operators") and job applicants ("Candidates") whose data is processed through our Service.

## 1. Introduction

AFRA is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal data we process. We provide a platform that automates the initial screening process for job applicants for our Operator customers. This Privacy Policy explains our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

## 2. Roles: Data Controller and Data Processor

For the personal data of Candidates, our Operators are the **Data Controllers** (or "Businesses" under certain US state laws). This means our Operators determine the purposes and means of processing Candidate personal data (e.g., for hiring decisions). AFRA acts as a **Data Processor** (or "Service Provider") on behalf of our Operators, processing Candidate personal data strictly according to their instructions and these Terms.

For the personal data of our Operators (e.g., business contact information, payment details), AFRA acts as the Data Controller.

## 3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data in two main categories:

### a. Candidate Personal Data (Processed on behalf of Operators)

When a job applicant interacts with an Operator's Instagram account using our Service, we collect the following personal data:

*   **Contact Information:** Name, phone number, email address (as provided by the Candidate).
*   **Application Information:** Availability, work experience, and responses to screening questions posed by the Operator.
*   **Conversation Data:** The message transcript of the screening conversation conducted via Instagram/Messenger.

This data is stored in our database and displayed to the Operator in a dashboard.

### b. Operator Personal Data (Where AFRA is Controller)

When a business signs up for and uses our Service, we collect the following personal data:

*   **Business Information:** Business name, email, Instagram handle, number of locations, follower band, hiring frequency.
*   **Account Information:** Contact person's name, email address, and login credentials.
*   **Payment Information:** Payment records (processed by Stripe; we do not store full credit card details).

## 4. How We Use Personal Data

### a. Candidate Personal Data

We process Candidate personal data solely to provide the Service to our Operators, which includes:

*   Automating the initial screening process for job applications.
*   Displaying candidate information to Operators in a dashboard.
*   Facilitating interview scheduling and follow-up reminders as instructed by the Operator.
*   Storing conversation transcripts for Operator review.

We do not use Candidate personal data for our own marketing, advertising, or other purposes unrelated to providing the Service to our Operators.

### b. Operator Personal Data

We use Operator personal data to:

*   Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
*   Process payments and manage subscriptions.
*   Communicate with Operators regarding their account, service updates, and support.
*   Comply with legal obligations.

## 5. How We Share Personal Data

We share personal data with the following third parties:

*   **Operators:** Candidate personal data is shared with the respective Operator for whom the application is being processed.
*   **Service Providers:** We use third-party service providers to facilitate our Service. These include:
    *   **ManyChat:** For managing Instagram/Messenger conversations and automated screening. Candidate conversations pass through ManyChat's systems.
    *   **Stripe:** For processing payments. We do not store full credit card details; Stripe handles all payment processing securely.
    *   **Meta (Instagram/Facebook):** As the platform through which conversations occur. Meta's own privacy policies apply to their platform usage.
    *   **Resend:** For sending email communications (e.g., account notifications, support emails).
*   **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights or property, or ensure the safety of our users or the public.

We enter into data processing agreements with our service providers to ensure they protect personal data in accordance with our standards and applicable laws.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. 

*   **Candidate Data:** We retain Candidate data on behalf of our Operators. Operators determine their own retention policies, but generally, data is kept for a period necessary for hiring processes and to defend against potential employment-related claims (e.g., 1-3 years). When an Operator instructs us to delete Candidate data, or when a Candidate exercises a valid deletion right under applicable privacy laws, we will delete the data from our active systems and instruct our sub-processors to do the same, subject to legal obligations.
*   **Operator Data:** We retain Operator data for the duration of their subscription and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for business operations and legal compliance.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

*   **Right to Access:** Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
*   **Right to Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
*   **Right to Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data.
*   **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of the processing of your personal data for targeted advertising or sale (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, please contact the relevant Data Controller (our Operator for Candidate data, or AFRA for Operator data) at the contact information provided below. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
