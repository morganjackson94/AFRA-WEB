# AFRA Candidate Data Notice

**Effective Date:** July 21, 2026

**To Job Applicants:**

This notice explains how your personal information is collected and used when you apply for a job through an employer using AFRA.

## What We Collect

When you chat with our automated assistant on Instagram/Messenger, we collect information you provide, such as:

*   Your Name and Contact Information (phone/email)
*   Your Availability and Work Experience
*   Your answers to screening questions
*   The conversation transcript itself

## How Your Data is Used

This information is collected on behalf of the employer you are applying to (the restaurant/hospitality operator). We use it to:

*   Help the employer screen your application.
*   Share your information with the employer for their hiring decisions.
*   Facilitate interview scheduling.

We **do not** use your data for our own marketing or sell it to other companies.

## Who Sees Your Data

Your data is shared with the employer you are applying to. We also use trusted third-party services (like ManyChat for messaging and Meta for the platform) to operate our service. These parties are required to protect your data.

## Your Rights

If you wish to access, correct, or delete your personal data, please contact the employer you applied to. They are responsible for your application data. You can also contact us at morgan@afravisibility.com for assistance.

## Opting Out

If you wish to stop the conversation at any time, you can typically reply "STOP" to the message.
